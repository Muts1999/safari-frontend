About Safari Diaries by Sammy

Passion for East African Safaris

Safari Diaries by Sammy was born from a deep passion to showcase the breathtaking wildlife, landscapes, and cultures of East Africa. Our mission is simple: to connect travelers with unforgettable safari experiences while promoting responsible tourism.

Why I Started

Growing up surrounded by East Africa's wildlife, I realized the world needed to experience the magic of this region. Safari Diaries is my way of sharing that wonder responsibly and authentically.

My Vision

To inspire travelers to explore East Africa, appreciate its natural beauty, and support local communities through sustainable safari experiences.
